# 知乎文章封面图 · 设计提示词

## 使用说明

以下三个提示词分别对应不同的视觉风格和生成工具，任选一个使用。


## 方案 A：抽象科技感（推荐 · 适合 AI 工具类话题）

```
A futuristic abstract tech illustration for an article about AI tools going paid in China.
Dark gradient background from deep navy (#0a0e27) to black.
Center: a glowing blue energy gateway / portal, with data stream lines flowing through it like a high-tech toll station.
The gateway splits into two paths — one side locked with a chain icon (representing payment barriers and VPN walls),
the other side open with flowing light (representing direct domestic access).
Subtle floating Chinese tech elements: abstract yuan symbol ¥ dissolving into digital particles.
Style: clean editorial illustration, not too busy, with plenty of negative space for article title overlay.
Mood: professional, slightly dramatic, conveying "the real barrier isn't money — it's access."
No text, no logos, no human faces.
Aspect ratio 16:9, suitable for article header.
```

## 方案 B：对比冲突感（适合知乎流量话题）

```
Split-composition editorial illustration. Left half: a frustrated developer sitting in front of a laptop in a dark room,
red error messages floating around — "403", "Timeout", credit card with a red X.
Dim red/warm lighting. Right half: the same developer, same laptop, but bright blue/cyan glowing interface,
clean terminal with green success output, a glowing gateway icon connecting to multiple AI model logos
(abstract geometric shapes in different colors — blue, green, purple, orange).
The two halves separated by a thin vertical line that dissolves into particles in the middle.
Style: modern flat illustration with subtle gradients, editorial quality.
Dark background overall, left side warmer, right side cooler.
Mood: from frustration to relief — the before/after of solving the access problem.
No text, no faces shown clearly (back view or silhouette).
Aspect ratio 16:9.
```

## 方案 C：极简数据风（适合理性技术读者）

```
Minimalist editorial illustration. Dark slate background (#0f172a).
A single glowing horizontal line across the center, representing a data pipeline.
Above the line: scattered locked padlock icons in muted red, floating in the upper half — representing barriers.
Below the line: 16 small glowing dots in different colors (cyan, emerald, amber, violet) arranged in an arc,
each connected to the central pipeline by thin luminous threads — representing 16 AI models accessible through one gateway.
Subtle fading Chinese text fragments in the background: 网络 / 支付 / 代理 — barely visible, ghostly.
Style: clean data-visualization aesthetic, architectural diagram feel.
Plenty of negative space at top for title overlay.
Aspect ratio 16:9.
```


## 推荐方案

**方案 A** 最适合这篇知乎文章。理由：

- 抽象科技感不挑读者审美，安全
- 「网关/通道」的隐喻和文章核心卖点（API 网关）直接对应
- 暗色调留白多，知乎标题叠加后不杂乱
- 不包含具体人脸/品牌，避开了潜在版权和偏见问题
